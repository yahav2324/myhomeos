myhomeos

myhomeos is a mobile app that helps you track household items and kitchen inventory, and organize shopping lists.

What the app does

  • Lets you sign in with Google to access your account.
  • Helps manage household inventory and shopping lists.
  • Shows item status and notifications inside the app.

This website exists to provide app information, privacy policy, and terms required for Google OAuth.
