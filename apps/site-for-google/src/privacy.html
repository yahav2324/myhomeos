Privacy Policy

Applies to the myhomeos mobile app. Last updated:

Summary

myhomeos uses Google Sign-In for authentication. We request basic Google account information to create and manage your app account.

Information we collect

  • Google account info: your email address, basic profile information (name / profile picture) as provided by Google.
  • App data you create: household/inventory items, shopping lists, and related preferences you enter in the app.
  • Technical data: basic logs for security and troubleshooting (e.g., timestamps, error messages). We do not sell this data.

How we use information

  • To sign you in and keep your session secure.
  • To provide core app features (inventory, shopping lists).
  • To improve reliability and fix bugs.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only when needed to operate the service (e.g., hosting providers) or if required by law.

Data retention

We keep your account and app data while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us.

Your choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can request data deletion by email.
